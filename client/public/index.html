HOW TO PLAY
THE SETUP

One or more players are secretly assigned the role of Spy. The spy does not know the location. Every other player knows the location and is given a unique role within it.

GAMEPLAY

Players take turns asking each other one question at a time. Agents must answer convincingly without revealing too much about the location. The spy must answer without giving away that they don't know where they are.

The active player chooses who to ask next. The host can skip a turn or start a vote at any time.

WINNING

Agents win by voting out the spy with a majority vote.

The spy wins by surviving the vote ‚Äî earning 1 point. The spy then gets one chance to guess the location: a correct guess earns +2 bonus points (3 total). A wrong guess keeps only the 1 survival point.

TIPS

As an agent, ask questions that someone unfamiliar with the location couldn't answer easily. As the spy, listen carefully ‚Äî the questions themselves reveal clues about where you are.
